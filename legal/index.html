Apple's videos

The macOS/tvOS aerial videos played by Aerial are produced by Apple and fetched directly from Apple's content-delivery network at first play, on each user's machine. Aerial does not host, mirror, or redistribute Apple's videos.

Community expansions

Each community expansion ships with its own license, declared in its manifest. Installing an expansion is opt-in. The featured expansion from Joshua Michaels & Hal Bergman is licensed under the terms at jetsoncreative.com/aeriallicense .

Privacy

Aerial includes no telemetry, no analytics, no crash reporting of any kind. The app makes outbound HTTPS requests to: Apple's CDN (for videos), this site's appcast.xml (for Sparkle update checks), and the expansions catalog when you open the Expansions browser. None of these requests carry user identifiers. Some optional features (Weather forecast via OpenWeather, Youtube streaming via yt-dlp, etc) may individually track you in some limited form, I recommend you check their respective privacy policies for more details.
