Aerial 4. is finally out 🍾 This took a long while, so a massive thanks to all the alpha testers who helped with their testing, bug reports and feedback !

If you are upgrading from the old Aerial Companion, close it in the menubar before install. After you download the app, move it to your `/Applications` or `~/Applications` folder and launch it, it will offer to migrate your data and cleanup the old versions. 

Auto updates are provided via Sparkle from now on. 

What's new :
- Add new pause on battery option for Wallpaper. Report what works/doesn't at https://github.com/AerialScreensaver/Aerial/issues/115
- Add new global shortcut to launch screensaver
- Tiny cleanup of popover UI, replaced "Lock screen" with "Screensaver", maybe it's easier to understand what it does
- Massive log cleanup
- More old files cleanup
- For more info on general V4 changes, check : https://aerialscreensaver.github.io/ 

Important : 
- If things don't work, open the app settings and look at the checklist on top. You should get buttons to resolve issues if any.
- You can start fresh if you get stuck by deleting `/Users/Shared/Aerial`. It resets *everything*, including settings.
