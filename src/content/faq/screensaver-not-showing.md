---
question: Aerial doesn't show as my screensaver in System Settings. What now?
category: Getting started
order: 16
---

Open the menu bar popover — if Aerial isn't your active screensaver, a banner at the top has a **Set as Default** button that registers it in one click. If the banner doesn't show, open System Settings → Screen Saver and pick "Aerial" manually.

**macOS Sonoma+ caveat**: macOS lets you pick a different screensaver per Space. Aerial only checks your *current* Space, so if you previously set a different one on another Space, switch to that Space and change it back from there.
