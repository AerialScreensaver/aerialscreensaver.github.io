---
question: If I quit the Aerial app, will the screensaver still work?
category: Getting started
order: 100
---

Yes. The screensaver itself is a macOS app extension bundled inside `Aerial.app`; quitting the menu-bar UI doesn't disable it. Some advanced features still need the app running in the background — automatic video downloads, weather updates, live-feed transmuxing — but the basic screensaver and wallpaper keep going on their own.
