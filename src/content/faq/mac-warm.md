---
question: My Mac warms up when Aerial is running. Is that normal?
category: Performance
order: 80
---

4K HDR video decoding is GPU-intensive on older Intel Macs. On Apple Silicon, Aerial uses full hardware decoding and the load is minimal — even at 240fps. If you're on Intel and notice heat, switch to a 1080p variant in Format settings, enable the battery-aware mode, and let Aerial auto-pause when most of the desktop is covered.
