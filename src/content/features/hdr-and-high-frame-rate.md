Apple's macOS 26 aerial videos include 4K HDR HEVC and a 240fps set that makes every pan and slow zoom look like film. Aerial supports them end-to-end, with proper Dolby Vision tone-mapping during playback and full hardware decoding on Apple Silicon. You pick the format you want as a global preference, and Aerial only downloads that variant — no wasted disk space.
