Expansions

Community video packs.

Aerial supports curated, community-made video packs called expansions. Each pack is its own source of videos with its own license and credits. The full catalog lives inside the app — open Video Library → Expansions to browse and install. Nothing is auto-installed without your consent.

Featured pack

Joshua Michaels & Hal Bergman shoot breathtaking aerial sequences over California, the American Southwest, New York, Monterey, and beyond — and have generously shared sets with the Aerial community since the early days. You can also support them by buying their commercial packs at magicwindow.app/aerial.

Want to publish an expansion?

Reach out via GitHub issues — we'll point you at the format spec and help with the catalog entry.
