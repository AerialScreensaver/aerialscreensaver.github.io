Compatibility

  • macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later
  • Apple Silicon and Intel
  • Single-display and multi-display setups

Updates

Aerial uses Sparkle for background auto-updates. You can switch between stable and beta channels in Settings.

After installing

  1. Open Aerial.app from /Applications.
  2. If macOS asks, allow it in System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  3. Open System Settings → Screen Saver and pick Aerial4.
  4. Back in the app, browse the Video Library and pick a few sources to download.

Stuck? Check the FAQ or open an issue on GitHub.
